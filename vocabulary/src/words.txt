Acquisitive (adj) - excessively interested in acquiring money or material things
Utilitarian (adj) - designed to be useful or practical rather than attractive
Intractable (adj) - not easily managed or manipulated
Zealot (noun) - a person who is fanatical and uncompromising in pursuit of their religious, political, or other ideals
Filiation (noun) - descent or derivation especially from a culture or language; filial relationship especially of a son to his father
Pithy (adj) - profound or substantial yet concise, succinct, and to the point
Notional (adj) - existing only in theory or as a suggestion or idea
Waver (verb) - to fluctuate between choices
Itinerant (adj) - wandering from place to place; unsettled
Pernicious (adj) - having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way; deadly
