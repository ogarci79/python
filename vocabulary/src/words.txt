Tangential (adj) - digressing; diverting
Gestation (noun) - growth process from conception to birth
Crescendo (noun) - steadily increasing in volume or force
Contumacious (adj) - rebellious
Diurnal (adj) - existing during the day; daily; of each day
Oblique (adj) - indirect or evasive; misleading or devious
Machination (noun) - plot or scheme especially to do harm; a secret political clique or faction
Neologism (noun) - new word or expression
Pungent (adj) - sharp and irritating to the sense
